Visibility Observer

The visibility of the footer on the bottom of the page is observed and a callback is triggered on changes.


Scroll down slowly.


Also
- focus another window (to "un-focus" the browser)
- switch to other tabs to observe changes (Note: your browser might disable transitions for background-tabs).

See the console for debugging.
